Donuts

Description

Deep fried dough in the shape of bagels with a punch of sweet.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 cup milk at 110 degrees
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon vannila extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1-2 quarts vegetable oil

Glaze

  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar, sifted
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Steps

Dough

  1. Put all the ingredients in the mixer
  2. Knead for 5-7 minutes
  3. Let the dough rise for 90 minutes
  4. Punch down on the dough to release the air
  5. Roll to about 1/2 inch thick
  6. Cut the donuts
  7. Let them rest for 30 minutes and heat oil 375 degrees
  8. Fry the donuts 2=3 at a time for one minute on each side

Glaze

  1. Whisk all the ingredients together
  2. Dip each individual donut while still hot into the glaze in all sides
