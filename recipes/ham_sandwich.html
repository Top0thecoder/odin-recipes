Ham Sandwich

Description

A tasty peice of deli between chedar and a slice of bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 slices of ham (deli)
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 peice of lettuce (optional)
  • 1 slice of tomato (optional)
  • Jar of mayonaise
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Spread as much mayo as necessary on the bread.
  2. Lay out the bread on a napkin.
  3. Put one slice of cheese on each peice of bread.
  4. Place the slices of deli on one peice of bread.
  5. Put the black pepper lightly on top of all of it.
  6. Put the two peices of bread together
