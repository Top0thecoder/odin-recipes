Pizza

Description

A greasy and apetizing dish, on bread, drizzled with marinara, and sprinkled with cheese and pepperoni!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 12 ounces grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • Flour (for rooling and shaping dough)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees
  2. Roll out the dough
  3. Add sauce all over the pizza
  4. Top pizza with grated cheese
  5. Season with black pepper
  6. Cook for 12 minutes
